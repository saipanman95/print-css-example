Saipan Island Zoo

Discover the plants and animals of Saipan and the Northern Marianas. Family-friendly exhibits, guided tours, and conservation programs focused on island ecosystems.

Buy tickets Visit info

Exhibits

Explore habitats and species that are native to Saipan and Micronesia, plus a hands-on marine touchpool.

Island Wildlife

See mammals, reptiles and invertebrates that call Saipan home.

  • Mariana fruit bat (flying fox)
  • Native lizards and geckos
  • Butterfly and insect displays

Marine Touchpool

Interactive tidepool environment with local marine species.

  • Hermit crabs and small crustaceans
  • Shallow-water fish and sea cucumbers
  • Educational tidepool talks daily

Aviary & Birdwatching

A sheltered aviary highlighting island birds and conservation efforts.

  • Local shorebirds and endemic species
  • Daily feeding demonstrations
  • Guided birdwatching walks

Conservation & Education

The Saipan Island Zoo runs local conservation programs aimed at protecting island habitats and supporting native species. Learn about volunteer opportunities and research partnerships.

  • Habitat Restoration
    Planting native trees and removing invasive plants.
  • Species Monitoring
    Partnering with researchers to track populations.
  • Education
    Workshops, school visits, and community events.
